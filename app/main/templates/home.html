Colby College Winter 2024 Recruitment Day

Colby’s Recruitment Day brings top employers right to campus, actively seeking Colby students for full-time roles and internships across various fields. This is your opportunity to engage with recruiters, gain insights into different industries, and explore roles that match your skills, passions, and career goals! All class years are encouraged to attend — whether you’re a senior pursuing a full-time position or a sophomore or junior exploring internship possibilities, this event is designed to help you succeed. Join us to make connections, gain confidence, and set yourself up for a successful future!

Joy to the Ville

Embrace the holiday and winter spirit at the seventh annual Joy to the Ville community celebration! Join Waterville Creates, the Colby College Museum of Art, the Waterville Public Library, and the Children’s Discovery Museum as we celebrate the magic of the season with movies, art making, play, Community Maker Days, and more! Free holiday treats to enjoy while supplies last.

Anxiety Toolbox Workshop

Feeling overwhelmed by stress, anxiety, or constant worry? You’re not alone. Join Julia Houska for a 4-week workshop where you’ll learn new perspectives and skills from Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT) to reduce anxiety and enhance your mental well-being. This workshop is designed to provide you with practical, evidence-based strategies to manage anxiety, build emotional resilience, and create lasting change in your college life. By the end, you’ll have your own personalized anxiety toolbox to tackle challenges with confidence and clarity.

Hill to Hill: Washington D.C. JanPlan Internship

Through the Hill to Hill internship program, the Goldfarb Center for Public Affairs selects and funds up to 10 students to live and work in Washington D.C. during JanPlan.

Ideate & Explore Grant 2024/2025

The Ideate and Explore grant is for early stage teams. It is intended to help with a preliminary exploration to find and validate an unmet need. We invite both for-profit and nonprofit ventures to apply. We also welcome you to meet with Halloran Lab staff to strategize how best to conduct the research and determine key assumptions. Halloran Lab will try to match grantees with Colby alumni and industry experts to assist with the research.
